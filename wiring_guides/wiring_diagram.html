🔵 3x Ultrasonic Sensors (HC-SR04) — Front, Left, Right

⚠️ EVERY Echo pin gives 5V! Must use a voltage divider (1kΩ + 2kΩ resistors) on ALL 3 Echo wires before connecting to Pi!
SensorTRIG Pin (Yellow)ECHO Pin (Green - via Resistors!)
Front US GPIO 22 → Pin 15 GPIO 23 → Pin 16
Left US GPIO 24 → Pin 18 GPIO 25 → Pin 22
Right US GPIO 16 → Pin 36 GPIO 26 → Pin 37

* Connect all VCC to 5V (Buck Converter) and all GND to Ground (Buck Converter + Pi Pin 6).

🟢 3x IR Proximity Sensors — Front-Left, Front-Right, Back

✅ IR OUT pin is 3.3V safe — connects DIRECTLY to Pi, no resistors needed!
SensorOUT Pin
Front-Left IR GPIO 17 → Pin 11
Front-Right IR GPIO 27 → Pin 13
Back IR GPIO 4 → Pin 7

* Connect all VCC to 3.3V or 5V and all GND to Pi GND (e.g., Pin 9 or Pin 14).

🔧 How to Make the Voltage Divider (For ALL 3 Echo Pins)

You need to make this for the Front Echo, Left Echo, AND Right Echo.
You need: one 1kΩ resistor and one 2kΩ resistor for each of the 3 sensors. 

  HC-SR04 ECHO ──┬── 1kΩ resistor ──── Pi GPIO Pin
                 │
               2kΩ resistor
                 │
               GND
